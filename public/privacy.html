Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

Friday ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our Chrome Extension and associated services.

1. Information We Collect

Google User Data: When you authenticate with Google, we access your email address and profile information to create your account. We also request access to Google Drive (read-only) to allow the AI to read relevant meeting contexts provided by you.

Meeting Data: We collect transcriptions and audio data from your meetings to provide AI summaries and Q&A features. This data is processed securely.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide core features: Transcription, Summarization, and AI Chat.
  • To improve our AI models (anonymized data only).
  • To maintain your account and provide support.

3. Data Protection

We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data during transit and at rest. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Your Choices

You can revoke access to your Google account at any time via your Google Security settings. You can delete your meeting history within the app dashboard.
